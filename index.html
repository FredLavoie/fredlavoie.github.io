About me

My name is Fred and I'm a software developer. I recently changed career to web development after working as a structural engineer for 9 years.

I have found my true passion in all things code. I'm obsessed with learning about how computers work, how Linux works, I get excited about command line tools and writing scripts. My goal is to become a knowledgeable and highly competent developer - I want to master my craft. I also really enjoy teaching and helping other to code.

Outside of computers, I love to stay active with CrossFit, weightlifting, biking & hiking. I also love to read - mainly books philosophy, business and biographies.
